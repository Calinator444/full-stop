Welcome to FullStop

How to Play

  1. You will receive a set of images in sequential order
  2. For each image you will be able to provide a guess as to it's location providing a latitude and longitude.
  3. After making a guess you will receive feedback on your accurracy.
  4. If your guess is 50 metres away from the location you won't score any points.
  5. If your guess is within 50 metres of the location the picture is taken you'll receive points, as well as a map showing the distance between your guess and the location.
